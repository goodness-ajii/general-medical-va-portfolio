## How I Work With Founders

### Onboarding (Week 1–2)
- Sign NDA and any required confidentiality agreements
- Workspace audit: calendar, inbox, CRM, file storage, password manager
- Stakeholder map: investors, partners, customers, advisors, board, internal team
- Tone-and-voice guide: how the founder communicates, what to escalate vs. handle directly
- Cadence agreement: morning sync, EOD recap, weekly briefing, monthly review

### Steady State
- Calendar and inbox managed daily during agreed hours
- Notes captured live or from recordings within 24 hours of every meeting
- Action items tracked in the founder's preferred system (Notion, Asana, ClickUp, etc.)
- Conference and travel managed end-to-end with daily prep packets
- Weekly executive briefing delivered every Monday morning

### Boundaries I Hold
- I never make commitments on the founder's behalf without explicit authority
- I never share PHI, financials, deal terms, or strategy outside the agreed circle
- I never operate outside the documented tone and voice of the principal
- I always flag legal, regulatory, or board-level items rather than handle them silently
