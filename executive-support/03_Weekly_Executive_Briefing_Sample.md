## Purpose

This is the document that lands in the CEO's inbox by 7:00 AM every Monday. The goal is for the CEO to read for ten minutes and then walk into the week knowing:

- The shape of the week ahead
- What to spend energy on, and what not to
- What I'm handling, what's blocked, and what needs them
- What's already moved since Friday

A good briefing replaces five Slack threads, three "do you have a minute?" check-ins, and the Sunday-night anxiety of an unread inbox.

## 10. Why This Document Matters in Hiring

When a founder reads a Monday briefing like this, they realize:

- I deliver **a single source of truth** for the week, replacing scattered Slack and email
- I separate **what I'm handling from what needs the CEO** — no one is silently waiting on a decision
- I track **commitments backwards (last week) and forwards (this week, next week)** so nothing falls through
- I protect **the CEO's energy** — flagging deep-work blocks, family commitments, recovery space
- I hold **a steady weekly cadence** so the founder never wonders if something is being missed
- I show **judgment, clearly labeled** — recommendations, not just status updates
