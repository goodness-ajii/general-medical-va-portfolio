## The Problem I Solve

In a typical medical practice, administrative tasks consume 30–40% of clinical staff time. Nurses field intake forms, the front desk juggles refill requests between phone calls, providers spend evenings catching up on documentation. That's time pulled away from patient care — and revenue pulled away from the practice.

If your team is drowning in patient emails, manual data entry, refill request triage, or insurance verification — **that's exactly what I solve.**

I become the dedicated remote administrative support that handles the operational load while your in-clinic team focuses on patients.

## How I Adapt This for Your Practice

When engaged by an actual practice, I follow this onboarding model:

### Week 1 — Foundation
- Sign Business Associate Agreement and complete workspace audit
- Replace placeholder content with your practice's real team, services, hours, and software
- Customize email templates and workflow to match your voice and policies
- Set up tracking spreadsheets in your existing Google Workspace
- Shadow the in-clinic team to learn workflow specifics

### Week 2 — Live Coverage Begins
- Begin handling assigned tasks during agreed hours
- First weekly performance report submitted

### Week 3 and beyond
- Full coverage of assigned scope
- Ongoing weekly reporting with KPI tracking
- Monthly SOP review and refinement based on practice feedback
